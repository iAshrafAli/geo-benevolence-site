About Geo Benevolence Foundation

Geo Benevolence Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives and creating sustainable change in underserved communities. Since our founding, we have been committed to addressing critical issues such as education, healthcare, poverty alleviation, and environmental sustainability.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through innovative programs, partnerships, and advocacy efforts that create lasting positive impact.

Our Impact

Education Programs

We have established 15 schools and provided scholarships to over 2,000 students, ensuring access to quality education for children in remote areas.

Healthcare Initiatives

Our mobile health clinics have served 5,000+ patients, and we've built 8 community health centers in underserved regions.

Environmental Projects

We've planted over 10,000 trees and implemented sustainable farming practices benefiting 500+ farming families.
